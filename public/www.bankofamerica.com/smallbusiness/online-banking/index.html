Go Paperless

You can save paper and avoid messy filing by requesting that we stop mailing your paper statement.

After you log in to Online Banking, look for the leaf icon ( ) or simply go to the Profile & Settings and click on the Paperless settings link.

We will send you an email every month when your statement is available for viewing online. If there are changes to account terms and conditions or other legal notifications, they will also be presented online, along with your account statements. You can always resume delivery of your paper statements at a later date.

Automatic alerts

Automatic alerts allow you to receive notices via email or text message based on conditions you specify, for example: insufficient funds in your checking or saving account, irregular card or account activity and balances approaching your credit limit or dropping beneath a specified amount.
