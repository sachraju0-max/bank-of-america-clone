Zelle®: A fast and easy way to send money

Whether you’re paying the sitter, settling up on a group gift or paying for your part of the pizza, all you need is the recipient’s U.S. mobile number or email to send money directly to their bank account.

  • There are no fees to send or receive money in our app
  • Money moves directly to their account in minutesadatext
  • You only need their U.S. mobile number or email address - your bank information remains privateadatext

To see how to use Zelle® to send, request, and receive money - even split payments - visit our Zelle® page.

Please note, Zelle® is intended for sending money to family, friends, and people you are familiar with. We recommend that you not use Zelle® to send money to anyone you don't know.
